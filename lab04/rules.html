The numbers guessing game has the following work steps:
A. Random 1 number
B.Get numbers and press Enter or click
C. Show numbers that have been predicted
D. If the predicted number is greater than the random number, the text is displayed too much.
E. If the number is less than the random number, the text is too small.
F. If the number that is matched with a random number, the text is displayed correctly.
G. There is a start play button to clear the number receiver. The group shows the numbers that were previously guessed.
And a box showing the results of the guesswork And go back to step a
